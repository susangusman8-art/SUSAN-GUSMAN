About Susan Gusman

The Strategist Behind Successful Books

My name is Susan Gusman and I have dedicated my career to one mission: making sure great books do not go unnoticed. I am a Strategic Book Specialist with deep expertise in author branding, book visibility, audience development, and sales strategy.

Over the years I have worked with authors across fiction, non-fiction, self-help, business, memoir, and more. I have seen firsthand what separates a book that sells from a book that sits. The difference is never just the writing. It is strategy, positioning, visibility, and consistent execution.

I started this work because I met too many talented authors who poured their hearts into their books only to feel invisible after publishing. That should never happen. Every book has a reader. My job is to make sure they find each other.

What makes my approach different is that everything we do together is handled through email. No rushed conversations. No vague verbal agreements. Every strategy, every plan, every update is written, documented, and saved. Full transparency. Full accountability.

What I Offer

Comprehensive Services Built for Authors

Every service is fully customized to your book, your audience, your genre, and your goals. Here is exactly what I do for authors who are serious about their success.

01
Book Visibility Strategy

Most books fail not because they are bad but because no one knows they exist. I build a complete, customized visibility roadmap for your book that identifies where your readers are, how to reach them, what platforms to prioritize, and how to position your book so it stands out in a crowded market. You receive a detailed written strategy delivered straight to your inbox.

02
Author Brand Building

Readers do not just buy books. They buy authors. Your personal brand as an author is one of your most powerful sales tools. I help you define who you are as an author, what you stand for, and how to communicate that consistently across every platform. A strong author brand creates trust, loyalty, and long-term sales that go beyond a single book.

03
Book Launch Planning

A successful book launch does not happen by accident. It is planned weeks or months in advance with precision and purpose. I design a full pre-launch, launch, and post-launch strategy that builds anticipation, drives sales on release day, and keeps momentum going long after the initial excitement. Every step is communicated and documented via email.

04
Audience Growth and Reader Development

Your audience is your most valuable long-term asset as an author. I help you identify, attract, and grow a loyal readership that is genuinely interested in your work. This is not about vanity metrics or empty followers. This is about building a real community of readers who buy your books, recommend them to others, and follow your career for years to come.

05
Content and Messaging Strategy

The words you use to describe your book matter enormously. A weak book description, a confusing tagline, or an unclear author bio can silently kill your sales. I craft compelling, conversion-focused messaging for your book title positioning, book description, author bio, and outreach so that every word works hard to turn interest into purchases.

06
Book Sales Optimization

Already published but not seeing the sales you expected? I audit your current book presence, identify the gaps, and build a targeted action plan to improve your sales performance. This includes reviewing your cover positioning, description, pricing strategy, reader reviews, and visibility channels. You receive a full written report with actionable recommendations.

07
Multi-Book and Series Strategy

If you are writing a series or planning multiple books, the strategy changes completely. I help you build a long-term author career plan that connects your books, grows your audience between releases, and maximizes the lifetime value of every reader you attract. Your entire body of work becomes a system that sells itself.

FAQ

Everything You Need to Know
Before Getting Started

Why is everything done through email? +
Email keeps every conversation documented, searchable, and accountable. When we communicate in writing, there are no misunderstandings, no forgotten agreements, and no vague promises. You always have a full record of what was discussed, what was decided, and what was delivered. It is the most professional and transparent way to work.
How do I get started? +
Send an email to susangusman8@gmail.com and introduce yourself and your book. Tell me where you are in your publishing journey, what challenges you are facing, and what you want to achieve. I will respond with a personalized assessment and we go from there.
Do you work with first-time authors? +
Absolutely. First-time authors are some of my favorite clients to work with because the opportunity to build a strong foundation from the start makes a huge difference. Whether you are pre-launch, just launched, or trying to revive a book that did not get the attention it deserved, I can help.
What genres do you work with? +
Fiction, non-fiction, self-help, business, memoir, children's books, thriller, romance, fantasy, personal development, and more. If you wrote a book and want more people to read it, I can help you reach them with a targeted, professional strategy built specifically for your genre.
How long does it take to see results? +
Most authors begin seeing meaningful visibility improvements within 4 to 8 weeks of implementing the strategy. The timeline depends on your starting point, your genre, and how consistently the strategy is executed. I set realistic expectations from the beginning and document every milestone along the way.
Is this only for self-published authors? +
No. I work with self-published authors, hybrid authors, and traditionally published authors who want to take a more active role in their book's visibility and sales. The strategy always adapts to your publishing situation and your specific goals.
What makes your approach different? +
Three things. First, everything is fully customized to your specific book, genre, and audience. Second, everything is done through email so it is all documented and accountable. Third, I focus on sustainable visibility and long-term results, not short-term spikes that disappear after a week.
Can you help with a book already published? +
Yes. Many of my clients come to me months or even years after publishing because their book never got the visibility it deserved. It is never too late to build a strategy and turn things around. A full audit and fresh strategy can revive a book at any stage of its life.
